Izzy's Top 5 Films

Here are my favorite 5 films of all time!

The Shining

Arguably Stanley Kubrick's BEST film, a must watch for all horror fanatics. Watch Jack Nicholson try to kill his family.

Mulholland Drive

A spooky lgbtq+ friendly mystery.

Pan's Labyrinth

A tragic and beautiful thriller fantasy Guillermo Del Toro film. Best creature design, definitely watch if you want a good cry.

Alien

Ridley Scott's most famous film. Another horror staple with sci-fi elements.

The Thing

This film inspired among us.
